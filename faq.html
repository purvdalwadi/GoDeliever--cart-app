Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GoDeliever.

Ordering

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse restaurants or food categories, select the items you want, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to be logged in to complete your order.

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets including PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. We do not accept cash payments for online orders.

Yes! During checkout, you can select the "Schedule for Later" option and choose your preferred delivery date and time. Please note that availability may vary by restaurant and location.

Delivery

Our delivery hours vary by location and restaurant availability. Generally, we deliver from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM, 7 days a week. Some restaurants may have more limited hours.

Delivery fees are calculated based on your distance from the restaurant, order size, and current demand. We also offer free delivery on orders over $50. The exact delivery fee will be shown before you confirm your order.

Once your order is confirmed, you can track it in real-time through your account dashboard. You'll receive updates via email and text message as your order is prepared, picked up, and delivered.

Account & Membership

Yes, you need to create an account to place orders on GoDeliever. This allows us to provide a personalized experience, track your orders, and ensure secure payments.

To reset your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact customer support.

Yes! Our GoDeliever Rewards program lets you earn points on every order that can be redeemed for discounts, free delivery, and other perks. Sign up in the "Rewards" section of your account dashboard.
